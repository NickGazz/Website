Sustainable Computer Mouse

The computer mouse is a product that is very familar and extremly prevelant in society. Most mice are ephemeral and easily disposed of. For that reason, it is an excellent example of a computer peripheral that can be redesigned and improved in order to promote a more sustainable approach to electronics.
