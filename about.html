Hi, I'm Nick. I'm a recent graduate from the Industrial Design program at Virginia Tech. A big focus of my education was human centered design and working hands on to create many prototypes in order to understand how a product will feel and function. Design is about more than the astetics, it is about the user and creating a product they will be delighted to experience over and over again.
